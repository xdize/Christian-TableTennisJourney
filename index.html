My Story

🏓 My Table Tennis Journey

I first picked up a paddle in 2012 after my dad built a table tennis table for my sister. Once I tried it, my dad noticed my potential right away. By the next year, I was entering tournaments without any formal coaching, and surprisingly, I took home gold in my very first one. Even without proper training, I kept winning gold and silver medals.

But after a heartbreaking, very tough loss in a crucial match, my mom took to Facebook to find me a real coach. That decision shaped me into the player I am today. Along the way, I met so many incredible friends who became family, and I will cherish these memories forever.
